How It Works

Two separate flows — one for drivers, one for passengers.

🚗

Driver Flow

  1. Register with Vehicle No. & RC number
  2. Provide Name, Age, Mobile, Pick location
  3. Email is tied to RC account automatically
  4. Login via RC-registered email + password
  5. Access driver dashboard & manage rides
Email linked to RC
👤

Passenger Flow

  1. Register with Name, Mobile & Email
  2. Login via Email OR Mobile number
  3. Access passenger dashboard
  4. Scan driver QR to verify & book ride
  5. Track journey in real-time
Email or Mobile login
